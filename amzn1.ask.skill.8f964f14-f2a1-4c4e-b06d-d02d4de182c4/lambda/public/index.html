• Amazon Alexa App - Rock Paper Scissors Online

Alexa Skill Information

My Alexa Skill is a simple game app, in which you can play Rock Paper Scissors with Alexa.

The app has 4 intents made for it, those being GameIntent (This one has my slot), ScoreIntent, InfoIntent, and YippeeIntent (This one is for fun).

GameIntent has 5 utterances, being 'choose {action}', 'i would like to choose {action}', 'my choice is {action}', 'i choose {action}', and '{action}', with {action} being rock, paper, or scissor.

ScoreIntent has 5 utterances, being 'score me', 'tell score', 'score', 'tell me my score', and 'what is my score'.

InfoIntent has 5 utterances, being 'tell me about this app', 'tell information', 'tell info', 'information', and 'info'.

YippeeIntent is a joke intent with a single utterance, that being 'what is your surprise'.
